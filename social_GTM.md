# RalphAI - Go-To-Market Strategy

## What It Does

RalphAI is a multi-agent marketing automation system for TODC (The One Digital Company). It orchestrates AI agents for campaign deep-dive analysis, marketing recommendations, campaign setup/review, and monthly reporting. It includes a React dashboard, Slack bot integration, JSON contract-based agent protocol, and browser automation for DoorDash merchant portal operations.

**Internal users**: TODC operations team, marketing analysts, campaign managers.

---

## Productization Assessment: HIGH POTENTIAL

RalphAI solves a universal problem: multi-platform delivery marketing is complex, repetitive, and data-heavy. Every restaurant group managing DoorDash, UberEats, and GrubHub campaigns faces the same pain. This is a strong SaaS candidate.

### Comparable Products That Got Productized
- **Ovation** (restaurant feedback tool) - started as internal ops, became SaaS
- **Marqii** (restaurant marketing platform) - similar domain, raised $4M+
- **Popmenu** - restaurant marketing automation, valued at $250M+
- **Revealbot** - ad automation started as internal tool, now SaaS

### Why RalphAI Can Win
1. **AI-native architecture** - multi-agent system is ahead of legacy competitors
2. **Vertical focus** - delivery platform marketing is underserved by generic tools
3. **Full-stack automation** - from data ingestion to campaign execution to reporting
4. **Already battle-tested** on real TODC operator data

## SaaS Go-To-Market Plan

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Multi-location restaurant groups (10-200 locations) using DoorDash, UberEats, GrubHub
- **Secondary**: Restaurant marketing agencies managing delivery for clients
- **Tertiary**: Ghost kitchen operators and virtual brand companies

### Positioning
> "AI-powered marketing autopilot for delivery platforms. RalphAI analyzes your campaigns, recommends optimizations, and executes changes -- so your team focuses on food, not dashboards."

### Pricing Model
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $199/mo | Up to 10 locations, 1 platform, weekly reports, basic recommendations |
| Growth | $499/mo | Up to 50 locations, all platforms, daily reports, auto-campaign setup |
| Enterprise | $999+/mo | Unlimited locations, custom agents, Slack integration, API access, dedicated support |

Per-location add-on: $10-20/location/mo above tier limits.

### Launch Channels
1. **Restaurant industry conferences** - NRA Show, FSTEC, Restaurant Finance & Development Conference
2. **DoorDash/UberEats partner directories** - apply to be listed as an approved integration partner
3. **LinkedIn outreach** - target restaurant group VPs of Marketing, Directors of Digital
4. **Content marketing** - publish ROAS benchmarks, delivery marketing guides (SEO play)
5. **Referral program** - existing TODC operators refer other groups for free month
6. **Restaurant industry podcasts** - guest spots on "Restaurant Unstoppable", "The Takeout Podcast"

### Launch Phases
1. **Phase 1 (Month 1-2)**: Dogfood with 3-5 TODC operators, collect case studies
2. **Phase 2 (Month 3-4)**: Private beta with 10-20 restaurant groups via warm intros
3. **Phase 3 (Month 5-6)**: Public launch with content push and conference presence
4. **Phase 4 (Month 7+)**: Scale with paid acquisition and partnerships

## Internal Adoption Strategy (Immediate)

### Current State Gaps
- Dashboard UI needs polish for non-technical users
- Slack bot has command stubs but may not be fully wired
- Agent reliability/error handling needs hardening
- No onboarding flow for new operators

### Adoption Actions
1. Run weekly "RalphAI office hours" with ops team to demo and collect feedback
2. Create 5-minute Loom walkthrough for each agent capability
3. Set up Slack channel #ralph-ai-alerts for automated notifications
4. Build operator onboarding checklist in the dashboard

### Integration Points
- DoorDash Merchant Portal (browser automation)
- UberEats Manager Portal (planned)
- GrubHub for Restaurants (planned)
- Google Sheets / Google Drive (report export)
- Slack (notifications and commands)
- Airtable (store mapping)

## KPIs

### Internal (Now)
- Number of operators actively using RalphAI weekly
- Hours saved per operator per week vs manual process
- Report generation success rate (target: >95%)
- Agent task completion rate
- Mean time from data pull to actionable recommendation

### SaaS (Future)
- MRR and MRR growth rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Monthly churn rate (target: <5%)
- Net Promoter Score from beta users
- Average ROAS improvement for customers (the money metric)
- Time to value: minutes from signup to first insight
